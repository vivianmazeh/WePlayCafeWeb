Welcome to Our Play Area!

We’re thrilled to have you and your little ones here. To ensure everyone has a fun and safe experience, please follow these guidelines:

General Guidelines:

  • Playtime Limit: Entry passes allow up to 3 hours of playtime per session and are valid for single use only.
  • Age Range: The play area is designed for children aged 2 to 12. Children under 2 are welcome but must be closely supervised by an adult at all times.
  • Anti-Slip Socks Required: Shoes are not allowed inside the play area. Everyone, including adults, must wear anti-slip socks for safety and hygiene. Anti-slip socks are available for purchase at the front desk.
  • Stay Healthy: If your child is feeling unwell or showing symptoms such as coughing, fever, or nausea, we kindly ask that they rest and visit us another day.
  • Supervision Required: Parents or guardians must remain on-site and actively supervise their children throughout their visit.
  • No Food or Drinks: No food, drinks, or gum are permitted in the play area to maintain cleanliness and safety.

Play Area Rules:

  • Slide Rules: Please always sit and cross your hands over your chest while using slides. Climbing up the slides or nets, or standing on equipment, is not permitted.
  • Trampoline Fun: One child per trampoline at a time, and no flips, tricks, or lying down while others are bouncing.
  • Wait Your Turn: Be patient and courteous to others. Take turns and share the fun with everyone.
  • Keep Valuables Safe: Leave personal items like phones, toys, or jewelry outside the play area to prevent accidents or loss.
  • Be Kind: Rough play, pushing, or shoving is not allowed. Let’s make this space enjoyable for all kids.

Safety & Courtesy:

  • Hand Hygiene: Please sanitize or wash hands before entering the play area to keep everyone healthy.
  • Zero Tolerance for Bullying: Aggressive behavior, inappropriate language, or bullying will result in immediate removal from the facility without a refund. Let’s maintain a positive and welcoming space for all.
  • Follow the Rules: Adhering to these guidelines ensures a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Membership Policy:

  • Memberships may be cancelled at any time
  • To cancel your membership, simply click the "Cancel Subscription" button in your confirmation email or contact our customer service team
  • After cancellation, membership benefits remain active until the end of your current billing cycle
  • Monthly memberships are non-transferable and restricted to individual households
  • A household is defined as children living at the same primary residence
  • Memberships automatically renew each month on your billing date
  • No refunds will be issued for partial months or unused time
  • Parents/guardians must accompany children at all times
  • Operating hours and availability may vary on holidays
  • We reserve the right to close for maintenance, private events, or unforeseen circumstances
  • Sharing membership privileges with non-members is prohibited and may result in membership termination
  • We reserve the right to cancel memberships for policy violations without refund

Thank you for helping us create a fun, clean, and safe environment. We can’t wait to see your family enjoy their time here!
